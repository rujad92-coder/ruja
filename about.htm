About BloodBank

Dedicated to saving lives through safe, voluntary blood donation since 1985

Our Mission

To ensure a safe, adequate, and sustainable blood supply through voluntary donation while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety. We strive to educate communities and create a culture of voluntary donation.

Our Vision

A world where every patient has access to safe blood when needed. We envision a society where blood donation is recognized as a civic duty and technological advancements ensure efficient collection and distribution.

Why Blood Donation Matters

Blood donation is one of the most significant contributions you can make to help save lives. Every two seconds someone in the world needs blood. Whether it's for surgery, cancer treatment, chronic illnesses or traumatic injuries, safe blood is critical.

Despite medical advances, there is no substitute for human blood. Blood cannot be manufactured and can only come from generous donors like you. A single donation can potentially save up to three lives.

Regular blood donors help maintain a stable blood supply, ensuring that blood is available whenever emergencies strike.
